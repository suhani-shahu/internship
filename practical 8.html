The Legend of Hanuman is an Indian animated series created by Sharad Devarajan, Jeevan J. Kang and Charuvi Agrawal for Disney+ Hotstar. The series produced by Graphic India premiered on 29 January 2021 with 13 episodes.

The series was renewed for a second season with 13 episodes on 27 July 2021. A third season with only 6 episodes was released on 12 January 2024.

"The Legend of Hanuman" Season 3 continues to delve into the epic narrative of Lord Hanuman. In this season, Hanuman sets his mighty foot on Lanka, unleashing his divine powers against the demon king Ravan. The season promises to bring to life the legendary confrontation between the heroic vanara and the formidable adversary in the ultimate battle of good versus evil.
